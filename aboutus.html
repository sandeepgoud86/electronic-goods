Our Identity

CORE ELECTRONICS Direct Ltd. is an started on april-2023 start-up having launched “Core electronics” - A unique & diversified Marketplace for Electronics Services products in India. Our Open Architecture platform offers hundreds of Financial product variants across Loans, Cards, Insurance, Investments, Payments along with thousands of SKUs across Electronics, Consumer Durables and Lifestyle products.

Our Vision

We wish to create a Financial Services Marketplace across Assets, Liabilities, Payments, Insurance & Investment in partnership with 50 Banks, Insurers & Financial Institutions by providing choice & convenience of 1,300+ Financial Products. Our ambition is to create World’s largest credit partnerships by leveraging our deep domain in credit space by offering White Label Loan & Card products. Along with this, we will also build an Omni-channel Lifestyle Marketplace Platform focused on Electronics, Furniture, Travel, Auto & Apparel. It will be a Partnership led model, which leverages our Platform & B2B domain capabilities, with a strategic partner responsible for fulfilment..
